​Part 1: The Spoken Prayer
​Father, let everything I do prosper.
​Heavenly Father, in the name of Jesus Christ, I come before you today with faith in my heart and confidence in your word. You are the God of increase, the God of multiplication, and the one who blesses the work of our hands. Father, I lift my voice to you today: Let everything I do prosper.
​Your word declares, "He shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water that brings forth fruit in its season whose leaf also shall not wither and whatever he does shall prosper." Lord, I receive this promise. Let my life be fruitful. Let my efforts produce results. Let nothing I do be wasted. The Lord will open to you his good treasure and bless all the work of your hand.
​Father, bless the work of my hands. Oh, everything I touch, let it prosper. Every effort I make, let it succeed. Lord, remove failure from my path. Remove frustration from my life. Remove every cycle of starting and not finishing. Your word says, "then you will make your way prosperous and you will have good success." Lord, give me good success—success that is guided by you, success that brings glory to your name.
​Father, anoint my ideas. Anoint my plans. Anoint my actions. Let your favor rest upon me. Let the favor of the Lord our God be upon us and establish the work of our hands. Establish everything I do. Let it stand strong, let it grow, let it flourish.
​Father, give me wisdom for prosperity. Guide my decisions, direct my steps. Your word declares, "Trust in the Lord with all your heart and he shall direct your paths." Lord, direct my path into success. Lead me to the right opportunities. Connect me to the right people.
​Father, let there be no wasted effort in my life—no wasted time, no wasted energy. Everything I do will count. Oh, everything I do will matter. Oh, whatever your hand finds to do, do it with your might. Oh Lord, give me strength and diligence. Oh, help me to work with excellence. Let my work stand out.
​Father, break every spirit of limitation, every force holding me back, every barrier to my progress—let them be removed now. Your word says, "I am the Lord your God who teaches you to profit." Lord, teach me to profit. Teach me how to succeed. Teach me how to increase. Holy Spirit, guide me daily. Let me not make wrong moves. Let me not walk in error.
​I declare today: everything I do prospers. Everything I touch increases. Everything I start succeeds. No more failure, no more stagnation, no more loss. Only growth, only progress, only success.
​Father, let doors open before me. Let opportunities locate me. Let favor speak for me. All things work together for good. Turn everything in my life for good—even my past mistakes, even my delays. Lord, let prosperity flow in my life spiritually, financially, mentally, in every area.
​I declare I am blessed, I am favored, I am prospering. Thank you, Father, because you are establishing me, because you are lifting me, because everything I do is prospering. I give you all the glory, all the honor, and all the praise. In Jesus' name, Amen.
